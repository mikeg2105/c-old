FLINT/2D: FLUENT PREPROCESSOR & TEACHING VERSION

FLINT/2D has been developed to meet two objectives:

  1. An easy to use program to facilitate the laboratory teaching of CFD on PC platforms,
  2. A simple 2D Grid Generator for Fluent and other CFD programs.
